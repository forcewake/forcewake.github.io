## What I Do

I'm a software engineer with a passion for building things that make developers' lives easier. My interests span across:

- **Software Development** - .NET, TypeScript, Python, and whatever gets the job done
- **Cloud Infrastructure** - Azure, containerization, Kubernetes, and cloud-native architectures
- **AI/ML** - LLMs, RAG systems, AI agents, and making AI useful in production

## About This Blog

This is my corner of the internet where I share:

- Lessons learned from real projects
- Explorations of new technologies

I believe in learning in public and sharing knowledge. If something I write helps you, that makes it worth it.
